# Opsis

This project gives you a super quick way to learn about things around you. Just snap a photo of an object, it'll analyze it and tell you what it is. It's designed to be simple and direct, getting you information without any fuss.

## Usage

Using Opsis is straightforward.

1.  **Navigate to the Home Page**: When you first open the application, you'll land on the home page with a welcoming message.
2.  **Take a Photo**: Look for the camera button located at the bottom right of the screen. Click on it.
3.  **Capture an Image**: Your device's camera will open. Take a clear picture of the object or matter you're curious about.
4.  **Get Information**: Once you've captured the image, the application will take you to the "Response" page where it will begin analyzing the image and providing information.
5.  **Capture Another Object**: After getting your response, you'll see a link to go back home and capture another object if you like.

## Features

Here's what Opsis can do:

*   **Direct Image Capture**: Easily capture photos using your device's camera right from the app.
*   **Real-time Photo Processing**: Your captured images are immediately processed and sent for analysis.
*   **Dynamic Information Display**: Shows "Analyzing image..." on the response page, ready for integration with an actual image analysis backend.
*   **User-Friendly Interface**: A clean and intuitive design for a smooth user experience.
*   **Responsive Design**: Works well across different device sizes thanks to Tailwind CSS.
*   **Custom Typography**: Integrates unique fonts for a distinct look and feel.
*   **Robust Routing**: Handles navigation between home and response pages, plus a custom 404 page for non-existent routes.
